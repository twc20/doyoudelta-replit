Battery Service & Replacement

Keep your vehicle starting reliably in all conditions

Reliable Battery Service

A dead battery is more than an inconvenience—it can leave you stranded. Delta Tire provides professional battery testing, service, and replacement to keep you on the road.

We carry quality batteries from trusted manufacturers, properly sized for your vehicle. Our computerized testing identifies battery issues before they leave you stranded.

Battery Services:

  • Free battery testing
  • Charging system diagnosis
  • Battery replacement
  • Terminal cleaning
  • Alternator testing
  • Warranty support

Signs You Need a New Battery

Slow Engine Crank

If your engine cranks slowly when starting, especially in cold weather, your battery may be weak and nearing the end of its life.

Battery Age

Most batteries last 3-5 years. If yours is over 3 years old, have it tested annually to avoid unexpected failure.

Warning Light

The battery or charging system warning light indicates a problem. Get it tested immediately to avoid being stranded.

Corrosion

White, ashy deposits on battery terminals indicate corrosion. This can prevent proper charging and needs attention.

Electrical Issues

Dim lights, slow power windows, or electrical glitches can indicate a weak battery or charging problem.

Frequent Jump Starts

If you need jump starts regularly, your battery or charging system has failed and needs professional attention.

Why Battery Quality Matters

Extreme Temperatures

New Mexico and Arizona weather—from freezing winters to scorching summers—is hard on batteries. Quality batteries handle temperature extremes better.

Reserve Capacity

Better batteries have higher reserve capacity, meaning they can power your vehicle longer if the alternator fails—critical in remote areas.

Warranty Coverage

Quality batteries come with better warranties. We'll help you choose a battery with coverage that matches your driving needs.

Cold Cranking Amps

Higher CCA ratings mean better starting power in cold weather. We'll match the right battery to your vehicle and climate.

Free Battery Testing

Know before you're stranded

Our computerized battery test takes just a few minutes and tells you the condition of your battery, alternator, and charging system. We test:

  • Battery voltage and condition
  • Cold cranking amps (CCA)
  • Reserve capacity
  • Alternator output
  • Charging system performance

We'll give you a printed report showing your battery's health and recommend replacement if needed. Testing is always free, even if you don't buy from us.

Get Your Battery Tested or Replaced

Don't wait for a dead battery to ruin your day. Stop by for a free battery test or call to schedule a replacement. Most installations take 15-30 minutes.
