Truck Accessories & Upgrades

Customize and protect your truck with quality accessories

Upgrade Your Truck

Whether you use your truck for work or play, the right accessories enhance functionality, protection, and appearance. Delta Tire carries a wide selection of truck accessories with professional installation available.

From lift kits and larger tires to bed protection and lighting, we'll help you build the truck you want. Our experienced team ensures proper installation and alignment for safe, reliable performance.

Popular Accessories:

  • Lift kits & leveling kits
  • Tonneau covers & bed caps
  • Running boards & nerf bars
  • Bed liners & mats
  • LED lights & light bars
  • Mud flaps & fender flares

Featured Accessories

Lift Kits & Leveling

Increase ground clearance for larger tires and better off-road capability. We offer 2" leveling kits to 6"+ lift kits with professional installation and alignment.

Tonneau Covers

Protect your cargo and improve fuel economy. Choose from soft roll-up, hard folding, or retractable covers to match your needs.

Running Boards

Easier entry and exit for lifted trucks. Stylish nerf bars and power-retractable steps available for most truck models.

Bed Liners

Spray-in or drop-in bed liners protect your truck bed from scratches, dents, and rust. Long-lasting protection for work trucks.

LED Lighting

Light bars, cube lights, and underglow for improved visibility and style. Perfect for off-road adventures or work sites.

Fender Flares

Cover wider tires and protect your truck from road debris. Bolt-on or pocket-style flares available in various finishes.

Lift Kit Packages

Complete lift packages with tires and wheels

Level + 33s

2" leveling kit with 33" all-terrain tires. Perfect for daily drivers who want an upgraded look without major modifications.

3" Lift + 35s

3" suspension lift with 35" tires. Great balance of on-road comfort and off-road capability for weekend warriors.

6" Lift + 37s

Full 6" lift with 37" mud-terrains. Maximum ground clearance and aggressive stance for serious off-roaders.
