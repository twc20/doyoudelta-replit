General Auto Repair

Complete automotive service for all makes and models

Full-Service Auto Repair

Beyond tires and brakes, Delta Tire provides comprehensive auto repair services. Our experienced technicians diagnose and repair a wide range of automotive issues, from routine maintenance to complex repairs.

We service all makes and models—domestic and foreign, cars and trucks. With our digital vehicle inspection, you'll always know exactly what your vehicle needs and why.

Our Repair Services:

  • Engine diagnostics & repair
  • Transmission service
  • Electrical system repair
  • Suspension & steering
  • Cooling system service
  • Exhaust system repair

Common Repairs We Handle

Check Engine Light

We diagnose check engine lights using professional scan tools and experience. We'll find the root cause and explain your repair options clearly.

Starting & Charging

Battery, alternator, or starter issues? We test the entire charging system and replace only what's needed.

Overheating

Radiator leaks, thermostat issues, or water pump failures—we diagnose and repair cooling system problems.

Suspension Noise

Clunking, squeaking, or rough ride? We inspect shocks, struts, bushings, and other suspension components.

Fluid Leaks

Oil, coolant, or transmission fluid leaks can cause serious damage. We find and fix leaks before they become major problems.

Unusual Noises

Strange sounds from your engine, transmission, or undercarriage? Our technicians can identify the source and recommend repairs.
