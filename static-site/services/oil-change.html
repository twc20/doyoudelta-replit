Oil Change Service

Keep your engine running smoothly with regular oil changes

Professional Oil Change Service

Regular oil changes are the single most important maintenance task for your vehicle. Fresh oil lubricates engine components, reduces friction, prevents overheating, and extends the life of your engine.

At Delta Tire, we use high-quality motor oils and filters, and follow your vehicle manufacturer's specifications. Every oil change includes a complimentary digital vehicle inspection to catch potential issues early.

Oil Change Includes:

  • Up to 5 quarts of quality motor oil
  • New oil filter
  • Chassis lubrication (if applicable)
  • Fluid level check and top-off
  • Digital vehicle inspection with photos
  • Tire pressure check

Oil Types We Offer

Conventional Oil

Traditional motor oil refined from crude oil. Best for older vehicles and basic driving conditions.

Change Interval: Every 3,000-5,000 miles

Best For: Older vehicles, low mileage drivers, budget-conscious customers

Synthetic Blend

Mix of conventional and synthetic oils. Better protection than conventional at a moderate price.

Change Interval: Every 5,000-7,500 miles

Best For: SUVs, trucks, vehicles with moderate demands

Full Synthetic

Premium engineered oil for maximum protection and performance. Best for modern engines and demanding conditions.

Change Interval: Every 7,500-15,000 miles

Best For: New vehicles, high-performance engines, extreme temperatures

How Often Should You Change Your Oil?

Follow Your Manual

Your vehicle's owner's manual has the manufacturer's recommended oil change interval. Modern vehicles with synthetic oil often go 7,500-10,000 miles between changes.

Consider Your Driving

Severe driving conditions (short trips, extreme temps, towing, dusty roads) require more frequent oil changes. We'll help you determine the right schedule.
