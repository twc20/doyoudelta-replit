AC & Heater Service

Stay comfortable in any weather—we fix what's broken

Climate Control Repair

New Mexico and Arizona weather demands a reliable climate control system. Scorching summer heat and freezing winter mornings require AC and heating systems that work flawlessly.

Delta Tire diagnoses and repairs air conditioning and heating problems. From refrigerant leaks to failed heater cores, we'll get your climate control working properly.

AC & Heater Services:

  • AC performance testing
  • Refrigerant recharge
  • Leak detection & repair
  • Compressor replacement
  • Heater core service
  • Blend door actuator repair

AC Problems We Fix

Weak or No Cooling

AC blowing warm air or not cooling well? Common causes include low refrigerant, compressor failure, or clogged condenser.

Strange Noises

Grinding, squealing, or clicking from your AC system indicates worn bearings, failing compressor, or debris in the blower motor.

Bad Smells

Musty odors when AC runs indicate mold in the evaporator. We clean and sanitize your AC system to eliminate odors.

Refrigerant Leaks

AC systems lose refrigerant over time or through leaks. We use UV dye and electronic detection to find and fix leaks.

Failed Compressor

The compressor is the heart of your AC. When it fails, the system won't cool. We diagnose and replace failed compressors.

Electrical Issues

Faulty switches, relays, or sensors can prevent AC operation. We diagnose electrical problems and restore proper function.

Heating System Problems

No Heat

If your heater blows cold air, you may have a failed heater core, stuck thermostat, or low coolant. We'll diagnose the issue and get your heat working.

Weak Heat Output

Poor heat output can indicate a clogged heater core, air in the cooling system, or faulty blend door actuator. We'll restore full heat.

Coolant Leaks

Sweet smell or wet floor mats? Your heater core may be leaking. This is a serious issue that can damage your engine if not repaired.

Temperature Control Issues

Can't control temperature or stuck on one setting? Blend door actuators and climate control modules can fail, preventing proper temperature adjustment.

Prepare for Extreme Weather

Don't wait for summer or winter—get your climate system checked now

Before Summer

Have your AC tested in spring before temperatures soar. Summer AC repairs can take weeks due to high demand. Beat the rush!

Before Winter

Test your heater in fall before the first freeze. A failed heater in winter is miserable—and dangerous in remote areas.

Get Your Climate Control Fixed

Don't suffer through another season with broken AC or heat. Our technicians will diagnose the problem and get you comfortable again.
