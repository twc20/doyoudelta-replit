VIP School Fundraising Program

Help your school while getting new tires

Support Your School with Every Tire Purchase

Delta Tire's VIP (Very Important Partner) School Fundraising Program helps local schools and organizations raise money through tire purchases. When you buy tires from us and mention your school, a portion of your purchase goes directly to support that organization.

It's a simple way to support education in our community while getting the tires you need anyway. No extra cost to you—just mention your school when you make your purchase!

How It Works

  1. Choose Your Tires - Select the perfect tires for your vehicle from our wide selection
  2. Mention Your School - Let us know which school or organization you want to support
  3. We Donate - A rebate from your purchase goes directly to your chosen school
  4. School Benefits - Your school receives funds for programs, equipment, and activities

Program Benefits

Support Local Education

Help fund school programs, sports teams, field trips, and educational resources in our community.

No Extra Cost

You pay the same price for your tires. We make the donation on your behalf—it's that simple.

Community Impact

Keep funds in our local communities and help build a stronger Four Corners region.

Is Your School Enrolled?

Most schools in the Grants, Gallup, Farmington, and Eagar areas participate in our VIP program. If you're not sure if your school is enrolled, just ask when you visit!

For Schools & Organizations

Interested in enrolling your school or organization in the VIP program? Contact any of our locations to learn about enrollment and how much your group can earn.

Ready to Support Your School?

Next time you need tires, mention your school or organization when you purchase. It's an easy way to give back while getting the products you need.
