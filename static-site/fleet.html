Fleet Services

Professional tire and auto service for your business vehicles

Keep Your Fleet Running

Delta Tire provides comprehensive fleet services for businesses across the Four Corners region. From small business vehicles to large commercial fleets, we have the expertise and capacity to keep your vehicles on the road.

With four locations and extended service hours, we're positioned to serve your fleet efficiently with minimal downtime. Our fleet program offers volume pricing, priority scheduling, and dedicated account management.

Fleet Services Include:

  • Commercial tire sales & installation
  • Fleet maintenance programs
  • Volume pricing discounts
  • Priority scheduling
  • Account billing & reporting
  • Preventive maintenance

Fleet Program Benefits

Minimize Downtime

Priority scheduling and efficient service keep your vehicles working, not waiting. Quick turnaround on routine maintenance and repairs.

Control Costs

Volume pricing on tires and parts, consolidated billing, and preventive maintenance help you manage and reduce operating costs.

Detailed Reporting

Track expenses by vehicle, service history, and maintenance schedules. Consolidated billing makes accounting simple.
