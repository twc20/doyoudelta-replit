Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

We accept both! You can schedule an appointment by calling or texting any of our locations, or simply stop by during business hours. Appointments help us minimize your wait time, but walk-ins are always welcome.

Most tire installations take 45-90 minutes depending on vehicle type and whether additional services (alignment, balancing) are needed. We'll give you an accurate time estimate when you arrive.

As a Tire Pros member, we offer competitive pricing on all tire brands. While we don't automatically price match, we encourage you to discuss pricing with us—we want your business and will work to earn it with fair pricing and exceptional service.

We carry all major tire brands including Michelin, Bridgestone, Goodyear, BFGoodrich, Continental, Yokohama, Cooper, Firestone, Hankook, Toyo, Pirelli, and more. We can help you find the perfect tires for your vehicle and budget.

Yes! We offer flexible financing options for tire purchases and auto repairs. Ask our team about current financing promotions and payment plans when you visit.

Every tire purchase includes mounting, balancing, new valve stems, and disposal of your old tires. We also include our digital vehicle inspection and the Tire Pros nationwide warranty with free 24/7 roadside assistance.

Yes! Tires purchased from Delta Tire include free tire rotations for the life of the tires (every 6,000 miles or as recommended). This is part of our Tire Pros Service Protection Plan.

We recommend rotating your tires every 6,000 miles or with every oil change, whichever comes first. Regular rotation helps ensure even tread wear and extends tire life.

Yes! We repair punctured tires when possible. If the damage is in the tread area and the tire is otherwise in good condition, we can typically patch and repair it. Sidewall damage usually requires tire replacement.

Absolutely! We perform computerized wheel alignments at all four locations. Proper alignment extends tire life, improves handling, and enhances fuel economy. We recommend an alignment check with every new tire purchase.

We offer conventional, synthetic blend, and full synthetic oil options. We'll recommend the right oil type based on your vehicle manufacturer's specifications and your driving conditions.

We service all makes and models—domestic and foreign, cars, trucks, SUVs, and light commercial vehicles. Our technicians have experience with everything from economy cars to heavy-duty trucks.

Yes! All of our work is backed by warranties on both parts and labor. Tires come with manufacturer warranties plus our Tire Pros Service Protection Plan. Specific warranty terms vary by service—ask us for details.

We accept cash, all major credit cards, debit cards, and checks. We also offer financing options for qualified customers. Fleet accounts can be set up with monthly billing.

Our technicians receive ongoing training and have extensive experience with all aspects of tire service and auto repair. As a Tire Pros member, we maintain high standards of service quality and technical competence.
