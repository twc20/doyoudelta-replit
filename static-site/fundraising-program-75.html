VIP Card Fundraising Program

Effortless fundraising that sells itself

Raise Money the Easy Way

Our VIP Card program is a win-win for everyone. Families get incredible value on services they already need, and your organization raises money without selling candy bars or wrapping paper.

Here's How It Works:

  • Cards sell for $75 (all processing fees added on top)
  • Your organization keeps $60 from every card sold
  • Supporters get real value they'll actually use
  • No inventory, no upfront costs, no hassle

What Card Holders Get:

  • 1 Free Oil Change (up to 5 quarts conventional oil)
  • 10% Off All Services for one full year
  • $50 Off Shell Leasing (new tire purchases)

These are services people buy anyway - now they save money while supporting your cause!
